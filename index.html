From the creators

Hey everyone,

We’re Andrew and Brian, the creators of AuditPap!.

In your life, it is very important to be on top of your finances. The foundation for this is understanding how you use your money.

This is why we created AuditPap!. We started with one question on our minds - how do we provide you with the actionable information you need to make smarter financial decisions?

This single question formed the basis for our journey, and now, months into it, we are ready to bring you onboard. With AuditPap!, you can expect:

- Accurate daily/monthly reports on your spending.
- Easy recording of spending.
- No ads. Never.
- Data privacy.

Right now, we are in the early access stage of release, and we will be giving access to select Android users to participate in testing and improving the app. Sign up to be the first one!
